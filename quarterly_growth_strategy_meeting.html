📋 Summary

Leadership reviewed Q2 performance: revenue grew 12% against an 18% target, driven partly by enterprise deals slipping due to procurement delays and added security reviews. Marketing traffic is up but conversion is lagging, prompting a proposed website redesign — pending an engineering cost estimate, since the team is near capacity with the August analytics dashboard. Customer retention remains strong at 94%, but rising support tickets tied to reporting performance and dashboard loading times were flagged as a priority. Hiring decisions and the conference budget were deferred pending Q3 forecasts and vendor proposals. No public commitment will be made on the August dashboard release date until a milestone review is complete. Open items will be revisited at next week's leadership meeting.
