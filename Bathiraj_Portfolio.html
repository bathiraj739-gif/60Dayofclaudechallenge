01 / About

Two kinds of growing, one careful process

I'm a third-year B.Tech Artificial Intelligence & Data Science student at CMS College of Engineering (2024–2028), currently holding an 82% average. I placed 1st at the Zonal Level of the Naan Mudhalvan Employability Hackathon, run by SM Groups and the Tamil Nadu Skill Development Corporation — three rounds deep, under real competitive pressure.
